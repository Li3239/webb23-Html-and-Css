Birman Cat Breed

Size Small to medium, with males weighing 7 to 10 pounds and females weighing 6 to 8 pounds.
Coat Medium.
Color Ruddy, red, blue, fawn
Characteristics The Abyssinian has a wedge-shaped, yet rounded head with broad ears and almond-shaped eyes in shades of gold or green. Her body is muscular and athletic, but slim, and supported by fine-boned legs.
Lifespan 9 to 15 years
Colors Abyssinians have what is known as a “ticked” coat, which alternates light and dark bands of color on each hair shaft. The warmth glow of their coat resembles wild cats like cougars. The coat comes in four primary colors: ruddy brown, red, blue and fawn. Some associations permit additional colors, however.
Shedding Weekly grooming is sufficient to maintain your Aby’s coat, but you may need more frequent brushing and bathing during shedding seasons to remove the loose hair faster.
